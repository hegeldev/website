We're active in responding to GitHub issues, but otherwise hang out in a few places. Feel free to come talk to us!

### Discord

We'd love to chat. Come join us in the `#hegel` channel in the Antithesis discord: https://discord.gg/yeggwH7vf2.

### Community-maintained libraries

There are a number of open-source libraries for Hegel. These libraries are maintained by the community, and are not officially endorsed by Hegel. Nevertheless, check them out:

* Hegel-zig: https://github.com/nickmonad/hegel-zig
* Hegel-java: https://github.com/aaronjeline/hegel-java
* Hegel-haskell: https://github.com/lambdamechanic/hegel-haskell
